our story

rooted in kansas city, brewed with intention

how it started

KC Roast Co. began in 2018 in a 400-square-foot garage in the Crossroads district. Armed with a secondhand Probat roaster and an obsession with the perfect cup, our founders—Maria and James—started roasting for friends, then farmers markets, then the whole city.

What started as a side project fueled by late nights and early mornings became Kansas City's go-to roaster. Today, we roast over 2,000 pounds of coffee weekly from our flagship roastery on Main Street.

what we believe

Great coffee starts at the source. We work directly with farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond—paying premium prices that support sustainable farming and fair wages.

Every bag we sell can be traced back to the farm where it was grown. We believe transparency builds trust, and trust builds community.

Our mission is simple: roast exceptional coffee, treat people right, and leave things better than we found them.

our values

the principles that guide everything we do

  • craft over convenience

    We roast in small batches, by hand, every single day. There are no shortcuts to great coffee—just care, attention, and a lot of cupping.

  • community first

    Our café is more than a coffee shop—it's a gathering place. We host local artists, support neighborhood causes, and believe in the power of bringing people together.

  • sustainable always

    From compostable packaging to carbon-offset shipping, we're committed to minimizing our footprint. Good coffee shouldn't cost the earth.
