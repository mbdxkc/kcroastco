our story

Born in the heart of Kansas City's Crossroads district, KC Roast Co. started with a simple dream: to bring world-class coffee to the midwest. Every batch is roasted by hand in our flagship roastery, where craft meets community.

We source our beans directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala—building relationships that ensure fair wages and sustainable practices.
